# Interviews

## Introduction
To better understand user needs and business requirements for FashionPanda, interviews were conducted with potential customers and local clothing retailers.

## Interview Objectives
- Understand customer shopping behavior
- Identify pain points in traditional fashion shopping
- Learn retailer challenges in reaching online customers
- Gather feature expectations for the platform

---

## Interview 1: Customer Interview

### Participant
University student (regular fashion shopper)

### Questions Asked
- How often do you buy clothes?
- What problems do you face while shopping for clothes?
- Do you prefer online or physical shopping? Why?
- What features would you want in a fashion delivery app?

### Key Responses
- Shopping takes too much time in physical stores
- Difficulty in comparing prices across shops
- Prefers online shopping for convenience
- Wants fast delivery and easy return options

---

## Interview 2: Retailer Interview

### Participant
Local clothing shop owner

### Questions Asked
- Do you currently sell products online?
- What challenges do you face in attracting online customers?
- Would you be interested in joining a fashion delivery platform?

### Key Responses
- No proper online system currently
- Limited marketing reach
- Difficulty managing deliveries independently
- Interested in joining a centralized platform

---

## Findings Summary
- Customers want convenience, speed, and variety
- Retailers need digital visibility and delivery support
- A platform like FashionPanda can solve both sides' problems

## Conclusion
The interviews strongly support the development of FashionPanda as a unified fashion marketplace platform.
